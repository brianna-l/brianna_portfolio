From scraps to significance

about

When we design something printed, we are producing a lot of waste through the making process; printing errors, changed design, backup copies, all of these are choices we made that ended up throwing things into trashcan or left in corners.

art & design

Every piece of material we use for printing and making is composed of natural resources, and by reusing we can create a healthy cycle to preserve these resources. We have the ability to turn unwanted materials into something useful again, so let’s start to practice this idea of remaking!
